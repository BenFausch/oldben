  • Vic

    Vic Stauffenberg is a freelance drumset and percussion performer based in Denver, CO. Vic has been drumming since 1994, receiving most of his education from graduates of University of North Texas. In addition to is work with MHBB, he also plays for Ryan Chrys & the Rough Cuts, voted best country band of 2015 by Westword Magazine. He has also recently supported Larry Nix, False Summit, and The Dollhouse Thieves live and in the studio. Vic has performed with Terry Bozzio (Frank Zappa), Dave Samuels (Spyro Gyra), Horacio “El Negro” Hernandez (Santana), Gilby Clarke (Guns ‘n’ Roses), members of Big Head Todd and the Monsters, String Cheese Incident, and Roger Clyne & the Peacemakers, and has shared the stage with Blackberry Smoke, The Marshall Tucker Band, Reverend Payton’s Big Damn Band, Saliva, Fuel, Junior Brown, In The Whale, Reno Divorce, The Delta Sonics, Cash'd Out, Cody Canada, Lincoln Durham, and Dax Riggs.
